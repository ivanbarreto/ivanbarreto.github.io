What is it?

Tool for designing and testing simple digital circuits or learn boolean logic.

Instructions

Click on the desired button and double click on the empty area below to create one instance of it. To connect the elements, simply drag the output black dot from the origin element to the input black dot of the destination element. To check if the result of your circuit, simply connect a LED element to the end of it.

Targeted audience

Anyone interested in learning boolean logic, digital circuits, or curious people in general.
